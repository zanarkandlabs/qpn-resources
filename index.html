Partner Sandbox
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • A Qualtrics brand provided free to partner orgs for building custom assets and testing early-access (EA) features. Administered by a brand admin within your partner org.
What you can do
  • Build and customize your own demo assets
  • Test new features in early access
  • Individual sandbox logins available per user
  • More flexibility than QDemo — QDemo assets are view-only and maintained by Qualtrics Demo Eng; your sandbox is yours to build in
Getting Access
  • Contact your Practice Lead or Brand Admin to create an account
Experience Community
qualtricsXM

  • Qualtrics' online hub for technical support, product knowledge, and industry connection. Ask questions, get answers, and engage with other XM platform users — includes a product forum (technical questions, tips, product ideas) and a leaders network (thought leadership, industry experts).

  • Access exclusive Partner-Only content

  • Link: community.qualtrics.com

Product Updates
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • A collection of resources to stay current on the latest Qualtrics product updates, releases, and early access opportunities.
Resources
  • Subscribe to Weekly Product Release Notes
  • Review Qualtrics Roadmap on Highspot, updated quarterly
  • Ensure you're on the QPN Email Distribution List — covers product updates, releases, and roadmap sessions
  • QPreview / Early Access (EA) Program: opt your partner org's brand into upcoming feature previews
  • Brand admin completes the opt-in survey (brand admin credentials required)
  • Brand signs the Preview Legal Agreement via DocuSign
  • Brand receives weekly recruitment emails and selects specific features to preview
  • Note: access is not open by default — brands must opt into each feature individually
