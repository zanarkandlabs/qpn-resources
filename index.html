Partner Portal
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • Your central hub for the Qualtrics Partner Network — manage your team, register leads, and access sales & delivery resources. It's also the main gateway to other partner tools including Partner Academy, Experience Community, and the ability to request QDemo and Highspot access.
What's available
  • Sales & Delivery Asset Libraries
  • Team management and lead registration
  • Access to Partner Academy and Experience Community
  • Ability to request QDemo / Highspot access via QPN Assistant (Practice Leads only)
How to access
  • Ask your org's Partner Portal Admin User to add you as a user
  • Don't know who your Admin User is? Ask your Partner Manager or file a ticket via QPN Assistant
  • Link: partners.qualtrics.com/login
QPN Assistant
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • Your primary channel for requesting support from Qualtrics Partner Success. Use it for escalations, renewals, program inquiries, admin support, and requesting access to partner tools.
What you can do
  • Product/Account/Renewals Escalation Requests
  • Partner Program Inquiries
  • Partner Admin Support
  • Request QDemo / Highspot access (Practice Leads only)
Who can access
  • All Partner Portal users — access most support options
  • Practice Leads only — can also access "Sales & Marketing Resources" to request QDemo or Highspot accounts for their team
How to access
  • Via Partner Portal → Support
  • Direct link: QPN Assistant Survey
Partner Sandbox
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • A Qualtrics brand provided free to partner orgs for building custom assets and testing early-access (EA) features. Each user gets their own sandbox login, which is also used to access Partner Academy and the Experience Community.
What you can do
  • Build and customize your own demo assets
  • Test new features in early access
  • Individual sandbox logins available per user
  • More flexibility than QDemo — QDemo assets are view-only; your sandbox is yours to build in
  • Your sandbox login also gives you access to Partner Academy and Experience Community
How to access
  • Each partner org has a designated sandbox Brand Admin — reach out to them to create your account
  • Not sure who your Brand Admin is? Start with your org's Practice Lead, or ask your Partner Manager / file a ticket via QPN Assistant
Partner Academy
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • Qualtrics' partner learning, enablement, and certification platform.
What's available
  • Certifications by product (CX / EX / MR) for the following roles:
  • Technology Consultant
  • Solution Architect
  • Account Executive
  • Engagement Manager
  • Solution Engineer
How to access
  • You'll need a Partner Sandbox account first — your sandbox credentials are used to log in
  • Once you have a sandbox account, two ways to log in:
  • Via Partner Portal → Resources → Partner Academy
  • Direct link: qualtrics.thoughtindustries.com → sign in with your sandbox credentials
QPN Highspot
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • Qualtrics' centralized content repository for the latest presales assets, GTM materials, product resources, and training content.
What's available
  • Presales: Latest demo scripts and assets
  • GTM: Qualtrics ICPs, customer plays
  • Product: Technical showcases, product roadmap
  • Training: Live session recordings
  • QPN program information here
How to access
  • Access is requested by a Practice Lead via QPN Assistant:
  • Click "Sales & Marketing Resources" → "Request access to Highspot for Partners" → enter the name and email of user(s) who need access
  • Once processed by Qualtrics IT, you'll receive an email invite to set up your login
  • No Practice Lead? Your Qualtrics Partner Manager can file the request on your behalf
  • Once your account is set up, two ways to log in:
  • Via Partner Portal → Resources → QPN Highspot
  • Direct link: qualtrics.highspot.com → enter your credentials
QDemo
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • Qualtrics' primary demo environment for delivering customer demos — built and maintained by Qualtrics Demo Engineering.
What's available
  • Gold-standard demo assets organized by product line, feature, and sales play
  • View-only access with minimal customization (expanded edit access coming soon)
  • Note: Healthcare demo assets are hosted in a separate brand called QHealth. Contact your Partner Manager for access.
How to access
  • Access is requested by a Practice Lead via QPN Assistant:
  • Click "Sales & Marketing Resources" → "Submit a request for Qualtrics sales demo assets or account access (QDemo)" → select "I need a partner account on QDemo"
  • Once processed, you'll receive an email invite to set up your login
  • No Practice Lead? Your Qualtrics Partner Manager can file the request on your behalf
  • Once your account is set up, log in at: qdemo.yul1.qualtrics.com

Product Updates
qualtricsXM
What is it
  • A collection of resources to stay current on the latest Qualtrics product updates, releases, and early access opportunities.
Resources
  • Subscribe to Weekly Product Release Notes — requires a Partner Sandbox account to log in and subscribe
  • Review Qualtrics Roadmap on Highspot, updated quarterly — no Highspot access? See the QPN Highspot section
  • Ensure you're receiving the Partner Newsletter — covers product updates, releases, and roadmap sessions — requires a Partner Portal account
  • QPreview / Early Access (EA) Program — opt your partner org into upcoming feature previews:
  • Brand admin completes the opt-in survey (requires sandbox brand admin credentials — no sandbox? See the Partner Sandbox section)
  • Brand signs the Preview Legal Agreement via DocuSign
  • Brand receives weekly recruitment emails and selects specific features to preview
  • Note: access is not open by default — brands must opt into each feature individually
